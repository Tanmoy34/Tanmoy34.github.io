project summery

Developed a multiplayer, open-scenario VR medical simulation in Unreal Engine using C++, in collaboration with my team at MedVrEducation, as part of an official office project featuring an AI-driven, ChatGPT-integrated interactive system. This scenario centers on John Simpson, a 62-year-old patient with lung cancer, exhibiting symptoms of febrile neutropenia and sepsis such as chills, weakness, shortness of breath, and confusion. Designed as a time-based, decision-driven simulation, it allows learners to interact dynamically within a photorealistic clinical environment—taking medical history, conducting physical examinations (HEENT, cardiovascular, respiratory, and extremities), initiating IV fluids, and administering broad-spectrum antibiotics and vasopressors. A key innovation of this project is the AI-powered NPC nurse system, where learners can issue voice or text commands to the nurse, who performs real-time medical tasks based on the learner’s instructions, fostering leadership and decision-making skills under pressure. The simulation is further enhanced with ChatGPT-assisted scenario logic that adapts to learner choices and provides contextual guidance or feedback during and after the session. My contributions included developing AI behavior systems, multiplayer communication logic, and adaptive event sequencing to create an engaging, intelligent, and realistic medical training experience.
